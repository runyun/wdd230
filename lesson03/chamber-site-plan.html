Yuanlin Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Overview

Site Purpose

This website is for people who might be interested in doing business in Yuanlin City and have a group to support it. The website provides information about members' business, event, and contact to promote their business.

Scenarios

  • How can I join this chamber of commerce?
  • What is my benefit if I join it?
  • How many people are in this chamber of commerce and who are they?
